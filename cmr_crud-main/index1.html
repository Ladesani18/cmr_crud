COMPUTER CENTER

The computer center is the central computing facility extended to all the faculty and students of the institute, apart from regular computer labs. There are two such centers with 100 computer systems each. This facility is also used to conduct Workshops, Seminars and online training classes. As part of research activities, these centers are extensively used by students for building of Apps, Projects and other Coding competitions.
